## Overview

As the 5-Day AI Agents: Intensive Vibe Coding Course with Google concludes, the capstone project is the final step for applying course concepts to a practical Kaggle use case.

Participants are expected to demonstrate at least three course concepts, such as:

1. multi-agent systems with ADK
2. MCP server usage
3. agent skills
4. security features and secure-by-default workflows

All participants receive a Kaggle badge and certificate by the end of July 2026.

## Key Details

1. Choose one of four tracks:
   - Agents for Good: education, healthcare, agriculture, arts, or related social impact domains
   - Agents for Business: enterprise workflows, operations, and measurable business outcomes
   - Concierge Agents: safe personal assistants for planning and everyday tasks
   - Freestyle Track: any useful, creative, or niche agent application with strong design
2. Submission format:
   - individual or team (up to 4 participants)
   - one project submission per person or team
3. Deadline:
   - July 6, 2026 at 11:59 PM PT
4. Winners announced:
   - by end of July 2026
5. Recognition:
   - top 3 teams per track receive Kaggle swag and social-media feature highlights
