# Problem Statement

People who work in AI cannot keep up with the volume of daily news across labs, tools, benchmarks, and policy.
The result is missed signals, repeated context switching, and low confidence in what matters.

## Target Problem

Create a daily agent workflow that converts many AI sources into one short, trustworthy brief in under 10 minutes.

## Why an Agent

The task needs repeated fetching, filtering, deduplication, and summarization under fixed constraints.
An agent is useful because the workflow repeats daily and benefits from structured tool calls plus explicit validation.
