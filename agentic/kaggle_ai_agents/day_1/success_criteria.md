# Success Criteria

1. Daily run completes in under 2 minutes locally.
2. Output contains 8 to 12 cards with working links.
3. No duplicate stories in top cards.
4. Each card includes a plain-language "why it matters" line.
5. At least 80 percent of evaluated cards are judged useful by the user.
6. Compared against `llm_pipeline` output on the same input window, quality deltas stay within 1 to 5 percent (source of truth baseline).
